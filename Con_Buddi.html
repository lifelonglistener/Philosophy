The Philosophy of Comparison

A crude comparison sets these traditions as enemies: "Existence" versus "Emptiness." But a higher philosophy sees the thread that binds them.

Both traditions posit that the ultimate goal of self-cultivation is Understanding.

"Confucianism finds freedom in the joy of practice. Buddhism finds freedom in the wisdom of non-attachment."
